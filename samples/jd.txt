Senior Data Analyst - Product Analytics
Acme Technologies Inc.

Location: Vancouver, BC (Hybrid)
Salary: $80,000 - $100,000 CAD

About Acme Technologies:
We're a fast-growing SaaS company revolutionizing how businesses manage their customer relationships. Our platform serves 10,000+ customers globally and processes over 1M transactions daily.

Role Overview:
We're seeking a Senior Data Analyst to join our Product Analytics team. You'll be responsible for analyzing user behavior, measuring product performance, and providing actionable insights to drive product decisions.

Must-Have Requirements:
• 3+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
• Advanced SQL skills for complex data extraction and analysis
• Proficiency in Python or R for statistical analysis and modeling
• Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
• Strong understanding of statistical concepts and A/B testing methodologies
• Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field

Preferred Qualifications:
• Experience with product analytics and user behavior analysis
• Knowledge of machine learning techniques and predictive modeling
• Familiarity with cloud data platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
• Experience with ETL/data pipeline tools
• Understanding of web analytics and event tracking
• Experience working in SaaS or technology companies
• Strong communication skills for presenting to executive stakeholders

Key Responsibilities:
• Analyze user behavior patterns to identify opportunities for product improvement
• Design and execute A/B tests to validate product hypotheses
• Create and maintain executive dashboards and automated reporting
• Partner with Product Managers to define and track key metrics
• Build predictive models to forecast user engagement and churn
• Conduct deep-dive analyses to understand product performance trends
• Present insights and recommendations to cross-functional teams and leadership

Technical Environment:
• Data Stack: PostgreSQL, Redshift, dbt, Airflow
• Analytics Tools: Tableau, Mixpanel, Google Analytics
• Languages: SQL, Python (pandas, scikit-learn, matplotlib)
• Cloud: AWS (S3, EC2, RDS)

What We Offer:
• Competitive salary and equity package
• Comprehensive health and dental coverage
• Flexible work arrangements (hybrid model)
• Professional development budget ($2,000/year)
• Collaborative team environment with smart, driven colleagues
• Opportunity to make significant impact on product strategy
